# Demo Script

This script is intended for a polished final submission walkthrough.

## 1. Opening

Open with the landing preview and the polished repository presentation in [README.md](README.md), highlighting the screenshot gallery and preview assets.

Introduce ArenaMind AI as a smart stadium operations experience designed for fans, organizers, volunteers, and on-ground staff.

## 2. Show the Role-Based Experience

Navigate through the landing experience and highlight the distinct dashboards for each role.

## 3. Demonstrate Fan Support

Show how a fan can ask for seat information, parking guidance, food, or route planning. Emphasize the local-response speed and fallback-safe design.

## 4. Demonstrate Organizer Oversight

Switch to the organizer view and highlight queue analytics, incident awareness, volunteer availability, and AI-assisted operational recommendations.

## 5. Demonstrate Volunteer Coordination

Show the volunteer dashboard and describe how task prioritization, emergency routing, and multilingual help support live operations.

## 6. Highlight AI Architecture

Explain the hybrid design: local answers for common requests, Gemini for more complex reasoning when configured, and deterministic fallback when the model is unavailable.

## 7. Close with Enterprise Readiness

Conclude by emphasizing accessibility, security, testing, documentation, and the platform's extensibility for future live-event deployment.
