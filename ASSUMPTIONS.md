# Assumptions

The following assumptions define the current scope and shape of ArenaMind AI:

- Stadium telemetry is simulated for the hackathon deliverable and is not yet connected to live venue systems.
- Gemini access is optional so that the experience remains usable in offline or partially connected conditions.
- Emergency control actions presented in the UI are illustrative demo interactions rather than production-grade operational controls.
- Authentication, authorization, and audit workflows are out of scope for the current build but are necessary for production command-center deployment.
- The documentation, screenshots, and demo flow are intended for submission readiness and stakeholder review rather than production-hardening completion.
