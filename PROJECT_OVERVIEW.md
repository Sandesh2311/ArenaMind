# Project Overview

ArenaMind AI is a smart stadium operations experience that demonstrates how AI can support high-pressure event environments through role-aware dashboards, multilingual guidance, and operational insights. The project is designed to be understandable, polished, and credible for a final hackathon submission.

## Business Context

In live-event environments, operators must react quickly to crowd movement, queue pressure, incidents, and service needs. ArenaMind AI presents a compact, simulation-based solution that helps users understand how intelligent assistance could reduce friction and improve operational awareness.

## Product Goals

For presentation purposes, the project is now documented with a visual preview gallery and architecture placeholders that make the repository feel more like a polished open-source product.

- Provide a clear, role-based view of stadium operations
- Deliver fast, useful AI assistance for fans and staff
- Demonstrate hybrid AI behavior with local-first and fallback-safe logic
- Present a polished, accessible, and submission-ready interface

## Target Users

- Fans seeking wayfinding, service, and schedule support
- Organizers overseeing crowd and incident conditions
- Volunteers coordinating tasks and communication
- On-ground staff requiring quick operational guidance

## Value Proposition

ArenaMind AI shows how a modern web experience can turn venue operations into a guided, intelligent workflow with measurable benefits in clarity, speed, and coordination.
